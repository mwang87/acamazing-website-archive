About Acamazing

Acamazing is a student-run co-ed a cappella group. Compromised of both undergrad and graduate UCSD students, the group is united by their love and passion for singing. Our repertoire spans the whole musical spectrum, ranging from carols to contemporary pop music.

Although you may believe we're out to make beautiful music, that is only half the picture. We use our music as the means to engage the audience in order to deliver a truly entertaining and memorable experience.


Founded: 2010

Audition Information

Audition's are done for the school year! Email us at acamazing@ucsd.edu if you have any questions or concerns, we are here for you!
